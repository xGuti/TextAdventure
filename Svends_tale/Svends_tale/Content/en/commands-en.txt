COMMANDS

look around		-	describe everything what player sees
go forward		-	walk forward
back			-	back
go left/right		-	walk left/right
go to 			-	go to
	temple		- 		temple (free heal or boost)
	blacksmith	-		blacksmith (buy / upgrade weapon)
	tavern		-		tavern (rest or talk with NPCs)
	shop		-		shop (buy supplies)
come to ...		-	walk to someone
talk			-	talk with NPC
use ...			-	use item
give ...		-	give item
attack			-	attack nearest monster or NPC
defend			-	defend from attack
dodge			-	dodge attack
try			-	roll a dice

Type 'back' to return to main menu
